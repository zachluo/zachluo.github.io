About me / bio

I am now working in Meituan. I received my Ph.D. degree from ShanghaiTech University in 2020, supervised by Shenghua Gao. I worked with Yong Jae Lee at University of California, Davis. I received my BEng. Degree from Shenzhen University in 2015.
