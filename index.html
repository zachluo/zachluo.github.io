About me / bio

I received my Ph.D. degree at ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Shenghua Gao. I worked with Yong Jae Lee (11/2018-5/2019) at University of California, Davis. I received a BEng. Degree from Shenzhen University in 2015, under supervision of Meng Yang.
